ABOUT US

We build software that people love to use

Founded in 2018, Nexora is a team of designers and engineers helping companies turn bold ideas into reliable digital products.

OUR STORY

From a two-person team to a trusted studio

Nexora started in a small apartment with two founders and one laptop. We believed that great software comes from listening closely to users and sweating the details.

Today we are a team of 40 people across design, engineering and strategy, having delivered 200+ projects for startups and enterprises around the world.
